**The V3 action score tool provides functionally equivalent coverage to V1 in all areas, with substantial improvements in diagnostic depth.** The only capabilities "lost" are numeric scores and independent virtue weights, which were intentionally removed per R6b and R6c as they lack source grounding and contradict V3's unity-of-virtue thesis.
