## Rule-by-Rule Assessment

### R1 — No Therapeutic Implication
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- Baseline is framed as "Baseline Philosophical Assessment" — not psychological assessment.
- Passion profile described as "philosophical self-knowledge, not a diagnosis, but a starting point for self-knowledge."
- Dominant passion framed as "Dominant Tendency" — not "Dominant Disorder" or "Primary Issue."
- Progress dimensions labelled: "Awareness of Passions" (not "Emotional Regulation"), "Quality of Judgement" (not "Cognitive Accuracy"), "Consistency of Character" (not "Behavioural Stability").
- Milestones framed as philosophical practice markers: "Clear Judgement," "Seeing Clearly," "Consistent Practice."
- Dashboard header: "Your Progress Profile" — not "Your Mental Health Profile."
- Milestone category names: "passion," "proximity," "practice" — no clinical categories.
- "Passion reduction" dimension labelled "Awareness of Passions" in user-facing text to avoid clinical connotation.
- Interpretation text uses "philosophical reflection" label, not "therapeutic guidance."

### R3 — Disclaimer on Evaluative Output
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- `BASELINE_DISCLAIMER` constant in baseline-assessment.ts: "This is a philosophical framework for self-reflection and does not consider legal, medical, financial, or personal obligations."
- V3FinalBaselineResult includes `disclaimer` field in the interface.
- Baseline page displays disclaimer below interpretation: `* {BASELINE_DISCLAIMER}`.
- Dashboard displays disclaimer below recent evaluations.
- API route stores disclaimer in the database row.

### R6b — No Independent Virtue Weights
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- No per-virtue numeric scores in V3 types or UI.
- V3 baseline assesses progress dimensions (passion_reduction, judgement_quality, disposition_stability, oikeiosis_extension) — not individual virtues.
- No virtue weights (0.30/0.25/0.25/0.20) anywhere in V3 code.
- V1 virtue bars removed from dashboard. Replaced with dimension level bars.
- V1 VirtueConstellation component no longer imported.

### R6c — Qualitative Proximity, Not Numeric
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- All dimension levels are qualitative: emerging, developing, established, advanced.
- Senecan grades are qualitative: pre_progress, grade_3, grade_2, grade_1.
- No 0-100 scores in V3 types, UI, or database schema.
- Baseline result card shows grade label, not a number.
- Dashboard shows proximity distribution and dimension levels, not numeric averages.
- Direction of travel is qualitative: improving, stable, regressing.
- Supabase V3 tables use TEXT CHECK constraints, not INTEGER ranges.

### R8c — User-Facing English Only
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- All UI labels in English: "Baseline Philosophical Assessment," "Progress Grade," "Awareness of Passions," "Quality of Judgement," "Consistency of Character," "Circle of Concern."
- Senecan grades displayed as English labels: "Before the Path," "Beginning the Path," "Overcoming the Worst," "Approaching Wisdom."
- Dominant passions displayed as English: "Craving," "Irrational Pleasure," "Fear," "Distress."
- Oikeiosis stages displayed as English: "Self," "Household," "Community," "Humanity," "Cosmic."
- Dimension levels in English: "Emerging," "Developing," "Established," "Advanced."
- Proximity levels in English: "Reflexive," "Habitual," "Deliberate," "Principled," "Sage-Like."
- V1's "Prokoptos" milestone renamed to "The Path Walked."
- No Greek or Latin terms on any screen.

### R9 — No Outcome Promises
**Status: COMPLIANT**

- Baseline does not promise that reaching a higher grade will produce specific life outcomes.
- Interpretation says "Your growth edge is..." — not "Improving this will make you..."
- Milestones framed as practice markers, not achievement guarantees.
- Direction of travel described factually ("Improving," "Stable," "Regressing") without implying that improvement guarantees specific outcomes.
- Dashboard does not frame passion reduction as clinical improvement.
- "Path of Progress" framing — not "Path to Success" or "Path to Happiness."
