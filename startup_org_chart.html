EXTERNAL
Accounting / Tax
External provider — connects to Sage-Ops
External Financial Compliance
  • Bookkeeping & reconciliation
  • BAS / GST lodgement (AU)
  • Tax planning & R&D incentives
  • Financial reporting & audit
Why external
Tax compliance requires a registered tax agent (legal requirement in AU). AI can prepare and categorise but cannot sign off. This is the one role that must have a human external provider from the start.
Interface with Sage-Ops
Sage-Ops prepares financial data, categorises expenses, tracks MRR/costs. External accountant receives pre-organised data → reduces billable hours. Sage-Ops receives accountant's reports → integrates into financial health monitoring.
When to engage
P1 (business plan review needs financial clarity) or when first revenue is generated, whichever comes first. R&D tax incentive eligibility should be assessed early — AI development costs may qualify.
