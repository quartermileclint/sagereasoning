# What Is SageReasoning?

SageReasoning is a structured reasoning evaluation API built on Stoic philosophy. It provides a universal reasoning layer that applies ancient principles of rational thought to modern decision-making, enabling both AI agents and humans to evaluate the quality of their reasoning.

## Core Product: sage-reason

The foundation of SageReasoning is the **sage-reason** endpoint, which implements the Stoic core triad:

- **Control Filter (Prohairesis)** — Distinguishes between what lies within your moral choice and what doesn't
- **Passion Diagnosis** — Identifies the specific false judgements that distorted your reasoning
- **Oikeiosis** — Assesses your alignment with virtue and rational nature

Rather than assigning abstract numeric scores, SageReasoning evaluates reasoning quality using **qualitative proximity levels**: reflexive, habitual, deliberate, principled, and sage-like. These levels describe how closely your reasoning aligns with Stoic wisdom.

## What Makes It Different

Most evaluation systems reduce complex human reasoning to numbers. SageReasoning instead provides **diagnostic feedback**—it names the specific false judgements (passions) that may have distorted your thinking, enabling genuine understanding rather than just rating.

The system treats reasoning not as a trait to measure, but as a process to understand. It serves professionals, developers, and organizations seeking to ground decision-making in rational principles rather than reactive impulses.

## Universal Application

Whether you're designing AI systems, training decision-makers, or examining your own choices, sage-reason evaluates reasoning across any domain: business decisions, ethical dilemmas, strategic planning, personal development.
