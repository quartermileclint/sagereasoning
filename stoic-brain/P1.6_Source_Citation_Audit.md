## Files Failing R7 (3 of 8)

### virtue.json — 16 sub-expressions missing citations

The four main virtues (phronesis, dikaiosyne, andreia, sophrosyne) and the unity thesis have citations. However, all 16 sub-expressions lack passage-level source references:

**Phronesis sub-expressions:** euboulia (good deliberation), synesis (good understanding), anchinoia (quick-wittedness), pronoia (foresight)
**Dikaiosyne sub-expressions:** eusebeia (piety), chrestotes (benevolence), koinonike (social participation), epieikeia (fair dealing)
**Andreia sub-expressions:** karteria (endurance), tharsos (confidence), megalopsychia (magnanimity), philoponia (industriousness)
**Sophrosyne sub-expressions:** eutaxia (orderliness), kosmiotetes (propriety), enkrateia (self-mastery), aidos (modesty/reverence)

**Remediation:** These sub-virtues are catalogued in DL 7.92-93 and Stobaeus Ecl. 2.59-60. Add passage-level citations to each.

### value.json — 22 indifferent items missing citations

The value hierarchy framework, selection principles, and Stobaeus three-locations framework have citations. However, all individual indifferent items lack passage-level references:

**12 preferred indifferents:** life, health, strength, beauty, intact senses, wealth, reputation, noble birth, freedom, friendship, knowledge, natural ability
**10 dispreferred indifferents:** death, disease, pain, disability, ugliness, poverty, dishonour, exile, low birth, loneliness

**Remediation:** The lists derive from DL 7.102-107 and Stobaeus Ecl. 2.79-85. Add specific passage references for each item.

### passions.json — 25 sub-species missing citations

The 4 root passions (epithumia, hedone, phobos, lupe) and 3 eupatheiai have citations. All 25 sub-species lack source attribution:

**Under epithumia:** orge, eros, pothos, himeros, thumos, menis, cholosis, plus others
**Under hedone:** epichairekakia, terpsis, kelesis, plus others
**Under phobos:** deima, oknos, aischyne, thambos, thorybos, agonia, plus others
**Under lupe:** eleos, phthonos, zelotypia, penthos, achos, plus others

**Remediation:** The sub-species taxonomy originates from Stobaeus Ecl. 2.90-92 and DL 7.111-114. Add specific passage references for each sub-species.
